Trail du Gypaète

The Association La Gypaète is a partner of a charity association: L'Association l'Orchidée, which supports and helps to realize the projects and dreams of seriously ill children throughout France: (www.lorchidee.org). This partnership , very important to us, helps raise funds to help this association fund children's projects. Everyone can, with their means, participate in this solidarity effort and you runners can subscribe to it when you register by deciding to add a few euros before finalizing your registration. Thank you in advance for your generosity.

La Cenise Bargy

La Cenise Bargy winter was created in 1993 BC, long before the Olympic Games. The race owes its name to the Cenise plateau and our symbolic mountain, the Bargy, where we draw our strength and our courage. Thus was born the sporting challenge where men and women challenged each other to prove their values!
