La Cenise Bargy

La Cenise Bargy winter was created in 1993 BC, long before the Olympic Games. The race owes its name to the Cenise plateau and our symbolic mountain, the Bargy, where we draw our strength and our courage. Thus was born the sporting challenge where men and women challenged each other to prove their values!
