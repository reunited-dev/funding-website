REACTIONS FROM BERNARD MOLLIET, PRESIDENT OF THE CMHC CONQUERING THE PEAKS A star Is Born ! From the merger of the hockey clubs of Chamonix and Morzine, the CMHC has just been born. By becoming professional, the Saxoprint Ligue Magnus has encouraged all its players to become professional. The quality and competence of all the players-hockey of our alpine cities made this merger necessary, promising for the future... Future for the radiation in the national sport of our two stations. Sporting future, with great ambitions to bring back the national title on our territory. Economic future, by involving the economic players of Haute-Savoie in our project. Political future allowing municipalities, departments, regions to help with a major project. Avenir jeunesse finally, and this is the most important, to offer our young people an example and a future. With the strength of our respective pasts, the Pioneers look to the future with serenity. All together, with the certainty of our Savoyard convictions, we will reach the heights. Then the CMHC will be great.
