How does it work?

It's easier than you think. You have 3 simple easy solutions :

Order/Subscribe Online

Watch Commercials

Order or subscribe online our partners' offers and earn tokens by making money for your club.

Watch online advertisements, answer questionnaires or sign up for a newsletter.

Play games offered by club partners and share your feedback.

When you send money to your sport club, you will receive loyalty Token from your club in return!

When you redeem Reunited Tokens and send money to your sports club, you will receive Loyalty Tokens from your club in return! These Loyalty Tokens can be redeemed for attractive loyalty rewards or instant prizes from your club. Each club creates its own rewards and prizes, but these can include match-worn shirts, ticket discounts, player meetups or other cashless experiences.
