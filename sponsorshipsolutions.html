>Who can use Reunited ?

Any sports associations or professional sports clubs that meet our creation criteria are eligible to launch a campaign on Reunited. We encourage you to browse our Reunited Resource Compendium and Creator's Handbook for tips on structuring and managing your campaign, as well as a comprehensive list of the resources we've made available to our creators. Also be sure to take a minute to review our rules. For helpful tips, creator testimonials, and other resources you might find useful as you begin to create your campaign, check out our category-specific articles in our educational resources for creators. When you're ready, just head over to our start page and start building - we're excited to see your projects come to life!

What should I consider when setting my funding goal ?

We encourage creators to research all costs involved in completing their project before launching. Your fundraising goal should include the amount you need to complete your sports project (costs of manufacturing and shipping rewards) and the costs associated with running a campaign on Reunited. Once you've launched your campaign, you can't change your fundraising goal or deadline. We've also compiled a list of services that can help you with your campaign fulfillment process.

How do I add rewards for my Donators ?

Once you've decided on rewards that appeal to your community, you'll find tools and options in the tab. reward, create add-ons, and organize everything for your delivery schedule. When creating rewards, we encourage creators to ensure that they set reasonable expectations for backers in terms of estimated delivery dates, the options they offer for shipping, and how the shipping will be charged. Creators can also use our tools to create NFTs.
