TRAIL DU GYPAETE REUNITED CAMPAIGN 2023

Help others through the gift of your time, donations

Chritelle Boisier
Organizer
Association La Gypaète
Charityble Sport Event
Started: 03.04.2023 Ending: 03.06.2023 Ojective Campaign : 30 000$ Reunited
Become Sponsors

The Association La Gypaète is a partner of a charity association: L'Association l'Orchidée, which supports and helps to realize the projects and dreams of seriously ill children throughout France: (www.lorchidee.org). This partnership , very important to us, helps raise funds to help this association fund children's projects. Everyone can, with their means, participate in this solidarity effort and you runners can subscribe to it when you register by deciding to add a few euros before finalizing your registration. Thank you in advance for your generosity.

This year La Gypaète enters the air 4.0 and will offer in partnership with the start-up Reunited an NFT of a bearded vulture created by artificial intelligence to all the people who will make a minimum donation of 10 Euros. Its NFT can then be exchanged or resold on exchange platforms by their owners and will be limited to 1000 unique copies.

The association La Gypaète and the start-up Reunited will also offer a totally innovative service for sponsors who will be able, with donors who are rewarded for their donations, to participate in a Reunited financing and sponsorship campaign with the aim of bringing together as many funds for the good cause. This innovative solution also allows the association to automate its entire sponsorship process and to offer a fully automated, simplified and secure sponsorship solution to its sponsors as well as totally innovative sponsorship packs, more adapted to the current markets and needs of brands and companies.
